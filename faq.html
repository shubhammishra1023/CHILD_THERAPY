Frequently Asked Questions

What is Behavioral Therapy?

Behavioral therapy helps children transform negative thoughts into positive actions. Our Visakhapatnam specialists use fun activities to teach better emotional control and confidence building.

How Does Behavioral Therapy Work?

Through simple exercises and positive reinforcement, we help children replace unhelpful patterns with healthier responses. Think of it like training the mind to focus on solutions rather than problems.

What Conditions Can It Treat?

  • Anxiety and worry issues
  • Persistent sadness
  • Fears and phobias

What Therapy Services Do You Offer?

We provide complete care including:

  • Speech development programs
  • Behavior improvement plans
  • Play-based learning
  • Autism support

How Do I Know If My Child Needs Help?

Watch for these signs:

  • Trouble speaking clearly
  • Frequent anger outbursts
  • Extreme shyness or fear

What Age Group Do You Treat?

We help children from toddlers (2 years) to teenagers (18 years) with age-appropriate therapy techniques.

What is Speech Therapy?

Our speech therapy helps children communicate clearly through engaging exercises that improve pronunciation, vocabulary, and conversation skills.

Who Benefits from Speech Therapy?

  • Late talkers
  • Stuttering children
  • Those struggling with schoolwork
  • Children with hearing issues

How Long Are Sessions?

Most sessions last 45-60 minutes, with games and activities to keep children engaged. We adjust based on your child's needs.

How Many Sessions Needed?

Progress varies, but many families see improvement within 10-15 sessions. We create personalized plans during your first free consultation.
