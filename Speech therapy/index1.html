🗣️ Speech Therapy Techniques for Children

There are multiple ways to improve their speech.

🎭 Speech Therapy via Puppet Shows

Symptoms & Solution: People with social anxiety and poor expressing(feelings or emotions) need this.This puppet shows help children engage in fun learning to mimic sounds and words.

📖 Speech Therapyvia Storybooks

Symptoms & Solution: Children with less vocabulary and poor sentence formation during conversations need to take this type of speech therapy which includes interactive storytelling and picture-based learning.

🕶️ Virtual Reality Therapy Games

Symptoms & Solution:Children who face difficulty in other traditional therapies are suggested to use VR learning which create real-life experience strengthening their speech in a fun,interactive way.

📱 Speech Therapy Apps

Symptoms & Solution: Poor grammar,pronounciation skills can be improved in an interactive way with some apps having daily challneges,rewards and voice-based learning for good communication skills/speech.

📦 Speech Therapy Subscription Boxes

Symptoms & Solution:It might be boring to stay engaged in continuous speech therapy sessions so we provide subscriptions and time limits to complete the guided tasks assigned, based on their progress.

🌍 Online Community for Speech Progress

Symptoms & Solution: Some people may gradually decrease attending their regular speech therapy sessions. It is due to lack of motivation/interest and feeling isolated in speech hurdles.So it includes progress to showcase their improvement and indulge motivation.
