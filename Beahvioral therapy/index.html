Behavioral Therapy for Children

Helping kids develop positive behaviors for a brighter future.

Types of Behavioral Therapy

1. Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA)

Recommended for Autism, developmental delays.

How it Works: Uses positive reinforcement to encourage desirable behaviors.

2. Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

Recommended for Anxiety, depression.

Therapy procedure: Helps children replace negative thoughts with positive coping strategies.

3. Parent-Child Interaction Therapy (PCIT)

Recommended for Tantrums, defiance.

Therapy procedure: Teaches parents techniques to guide children's behavior.

4.virtual reality behavioral therapy

Recommended for people suffering from Anxiety,PTSD,phobias

Therapy procedure: It uses VR simulations to expose individuals to real world scenarios.

5.DIY Behavioral Therapy

Recommended for People suffering from stress,mild anxiety or seeking self-help approach.

Therapy procedure: Enhances book reading,behavioral exercises,meditation and therapy worksheets(Q/Ans)

6. Behavior Therapy for Parenting

Recommended for Child tantrums,ADHD,emotional imbalance issues.

Therapy procedure: It includes parent-Child Interaction Therapy(PCIT),teaches parents about the discipline techniques.

7.Mindfulness Based-Behavioral Therapy

Recommended for Suffring from negative thought patterns,Depression,Emotional irregulation and OCD,ADHD as well.

Therapy procedure: It uses Cognitive Behavioral Therapy(CBT),focuses on improving ones awareness,present-moment focus and mindful meditation.

8.Gamified Behavioral Therapy

Recommended for Autsim and children suffering from learning disabilities.

Therapy procedure: Encourages self-expression through art to process emotions.

9.Art Therapy as Behavioral Therapy

Recommended for Children with emotional distress, trauma, or anxiety.

Therapy procedure: Using Quizes,Exercises with rewards,worksheets,Progress tracking.

10.Innovative low-cost Podcast Behavioral Therapy

Recommended for People who prefer audio content and it is a self-paced mental health support .

Therapy procedure: It helps storytelling which helps some other few people seeking for guidance.
