Our Areas of Expertise

Behavioral Therapy
Dr. Emily Thompson, BCBA
Personalized support for children facing ADHD, autism spectrum challenges, or behavioral difficulties. Our team uses proven Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) methods tailored to each child's unique needs, helping them build social confidence, manage emotions, and develop practical life skills through engaging activities.
Speech & Language Therapy
Sarah Johnson, MS, CCC-SLP
Customized programs to help children overcome speech delays, articulation issues, or social communication barriers. We combine fun, play-based methods with collaborative parent training to empower families to support progress at home.
Occupational Therapy

Sensory-friendly strategies to improve motor skills, sensory processing, and independence in everyday tasks . Our therapists help children master dressing, writing, and self-care while boosting confidence in daily interactions.
